About me

I received my B.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from the Honors School, Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2015, and my M.Sc. in Mechatronics from the Department of Machine Design, Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) in 2016. My master thesis was supervised by Prof. Lei Feng.

In Auguest 2017, I started my Ph.D. studies in the Division of Decision and Control Systems, KTH, under supervision of Prof. Jonas Mårtensson and Prof. Karl H. Johansson.

My research interests include optimal control, reinforcement learning, and their application to transportation systems.
